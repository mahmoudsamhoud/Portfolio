RETAILER PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

Introduction

Mega Mart is a multinational corporation that operates a chain of retail and grocery stores. Mega Mart management is planning to acquire another retailer to expand its market share. The company has provided Mega Mart with several tables relating to their customers and sales to be analyzed by Mega Mart to gain insights and to be able to decide whether to acquire this company or not.

Objectives

Mega Mart management need to get insights into

  • Overall statistics (KPIs):

    • The total sales

    • Total discount

    • Overall percentage discount

    • Avg discount percentage

    • Total quantity sold

    • Max quantity sold in a single transaction

    • Total sales value per basket

    • Total sales value per household

  • Monthly Trend for Sales

  • Weekly Trend for sales

  • 2016 sales vs 2017 sales

  • Household:

    • The distribution of total sales value purchased at the household level.

    • Top 10 households by sales value

    • Top 10 households by quantity purchased

    • Total sales value for our top 10 households by value, ordered from highest to lowest.

  • Products:

    • Products that had the most sales by sales value

    • Did the top selling items have a higher avg discount rate?

    • Top 10 products by sales

    • The item that had the highest quantity sold in a single transaction.

  • Demographics:

    • Sales by age_desc

    • Sales by income_desc.

    • Mean household sales by AGE_DESC and HH_COMP_DESC.

Also we would like to visualize various aspects of our pizza sales data to gain insights and understand key trends.

  • Daily Trend for Total Orders

  • Weekly Trend for Total Orders

  • Monthly Trend for Total Orders

  • Percentage of Sales by Pizza Category

  • Percentage of Sales by Pizza Size

  • Total Pizzas Sold by Pizza Category

  • Top 5 Sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity and Total Orders

  • Lowest 5 Sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity and Total Orders

Analysis Method

I used Python to read in multiple flat files, down cast some data fields to reduce memory usage, join tables to provide a single source of information, shape and aggregate sales data to calculate KPIs, and visualize the data to communicate findings.
