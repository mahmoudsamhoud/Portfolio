AdvnatureWorks Sales Dashboard

Introduction

Adventure Works Cycles is a fictitious manufacturing company that produces and distributes metal and composite bicycles to commercial markets in North America, Europe, and Asia. The headquarters for Adventure Works Cycles is Bothell, Washington, where the company employs 500 workers. Additionally, Adventure Works Cycles employs several regional sales teams throughout its market base.

Objectives

The aim of this project is to gain insights into the following:

  • Track KPIs (Orders, revenue, returns).

  • Compare regional performance.

  • Analyze product-level trends.

  • Identify high-value customers.

About Dataset

The data used in this project are 10 CSV files that contain 3 categories of data:

  1. Customer data (name, username, birthdate, marital status, annual income, gender, etc...)
  2. Product data (name, code, size, model, price, color, main product category, subcategory, etc...)
  3. Sales data for three years 2015, 2016, 2017 (order date, order number, buyer, order quantity, territory, etc...)

Analysis Method

The data files were transformed and processed using Power BI to ensure data completeness and validity. A relational data model was built to facilitate efficient management, retrieval and analysis. New columns and measures were added to help with the analysis. Finally, the dashboard was created to help client track KPIs (sales, revenue, returns), compare regional performances, analyze product-level trends and forecasts, and identify high-level customers.

Resulting Dashboard

The dashboard has three pages:

  1. Executive summary page from which we can track:

    • Monthly revenue, monthly orders, monthly returns items for current month compared to previous month

    • Top product in terms of orders as well as a list of all products ordered by total orders and the number of returns

    • Top product in terms of profit

    • Total orders for each category of products

    • Total orders for subcategories of each main category

    • Reginal performance for three regions, North America, Europe, and Pacific.



  2. Product page from which we zoom into a single product and track:

    • Current month orders vs previous month

    • Current month revenue vs previous month

    • Current month returns vs previous month

    • Weekly profit and returns



  3. Customer detail page from which we can track:

    • Top 100 customers in terms of revenue

    • Total orders for each gender

    • Total orders of different income levels for customers

    • Total orders by customers’ age

    • Name of top customer and the corresponding number of orders and revenue.
